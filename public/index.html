How to play (2-minute read)

The story: 4–6 of you are stranded on the freighter Meridian. Earth is silent. Five crises stand between you and rescue.

Your AI: Each player has a private AI assistant that briefs you the moment a crisis appears — and answers up to 3 follow-up questions. But the crash damaged the AI core: with 4 players, one assistant is corrupted; with 5–6 players, two are. A corrupted AI lies convincingly, and its owner has no idea.

The traitor: One PLAYER is secretly the saboteur. Their AI knows it, tells them the real answers, and coaches their lies.

Each round: read the crisis and your AI's briefing, argue in the crew chat about whose AI to trust, then vote. When everyone has voted, the outcome shows and the next crisis follows automatically. Wrong choices burn oxygen — run out, and the saboteur wins.

The ending: survive all 5 crises and the rescue ship arrives — then everyone votes twice: who was the saboteur, and whose AI was corrupted. Name the right saboteur and the crew wins. Get it wrong, and they walk free.
